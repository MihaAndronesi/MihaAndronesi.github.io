Packing Tips

Tip Details
Roll, Don’t Fold Rolling clothes saves space and minimizes wrinkles compared to folding them.
Pack Versatile Clothing Choose clothes that can be mixed and matched for multiple outfits, reducing the number of items you need to pack.
Leave Room for Souvenirs Don’t overpack—leave some space in your luggage for souvenirs or items you might pick up during your trip.
